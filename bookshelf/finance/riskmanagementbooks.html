  • Plight of the Fortune Tellers
  • Riccardo Rebonato
  • This is a discussion of some fundamental issues with current risk management practices especially with too-precise estimation of tail risks. The author does not claim to have an answer to the issue but is very honest about the statistical limitations of many potential "solutions". It makes us step back and think about the nature of financial risk and assess our ability to manage it.

Let $X \sim{} U(0, 1) $ and $Y = X^n $.
Get $f_Y(y)$.
\begin{align} F_Y(y) &= P(Y \leq y) \\ & = P(X^n \leq y) \\ & = P(X \leq y^{\frac{1}{n}}) \\ & = F_X(y^{\frac{1}{n}}) \\ & = y^{\frac{1}{n}} \qquad \text{because } F_X(x) = x \\ \end{align} Hence, $$ f_Y(y) = \frac{F_Y(y)}{dy}= \frac{1}{n}y^{\frac{1}{n}-1} \qquad 0\leq y \leq 1 $$

Let $X, Y, Z$ be independent $r.v. \sim U(0,1)$.
Find $P(Z \geq XY)$.
\begin{align} P(Z \geq XY) &= \iiint \limits_{z\geq xy} f_{X,Y,Z}(x,y,z) dx dy dz \\ & = \int_{xy}^1 \int_0^1 \int_0^1 1\cdot 1\cdot 1 dx dy dz \\ & = \int_0^1 \int_0^1 \int_{xy}^1 dz dx dy \\ & = \int_0^1 \int_0^1 (1-xy) dx dy \\ & = \int_0^1 (x - y\frac{x^2}{2})\bigg|_0^1 dy = \int_0^1(1-\frac{y}{2})dy \\ & = \left(y - \frac{y^2}{4} \right) \bigg|_0^1 \\ & = 1 - \frac{1}{4} = \frac{3}{4} \end{align}
